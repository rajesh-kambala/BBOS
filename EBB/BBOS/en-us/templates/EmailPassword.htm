Thank you for recently inquiring on Blue Book Online Services (BBOS). BBOS connects eligible Members to important Blue Book information 24 hours/day, 7 days/week. To access BBOS, point your web browser to http://apps.bluebookservices.com/bbos.

Quick Tips:

  • Change your password to something simple for you to remember.
    [Go to "My Account" > "User Profile," remember to click "Save" when done.]
  • Search by company name or BBID through Quick Find. In the upper right corner of every screen, you can type in the name of the company you are looking for. [Tip: If you don't know how to spell the whole company name, just type the name partially and matches will display in a drop down menu.]
  • We encourage you to take a few minutes and just explore the site; there are valuable tools and information at every turn.

Further Assistance:

  • A comprehensive Help Guide is available on BBOS; just click the question mark icon, which is found on every page within the site.
  • Our Customer Service professionals are here from 7:30 AM - 5:00 PM (Central Time), Monday thru Friday to provide you with assistance.
  • Training Webinars are available to you. If you would like to attend one, please email us.
